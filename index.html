The premise

What should Australia become, and who is building it?

Future Australiana is a long-form interview show exploring the forces shaping Australia's next chapter: quantum computing, AI, space, advanced manufacturing, energy, finance, infrastructure, faith, culture and public policy.

The tone is serious but accessible: plainspoken conversations with founders, investors, scientists, operators, public leaders and thinkers about what Australia can build, back and become.
