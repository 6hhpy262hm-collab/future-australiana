The premise

What should Australia become, and who is building it?

Future Australiana is a long-form interview show exploring the forces shaping Australia's next chapter: frontier technology, industry, capital, science, energy, infrastructure, culture, faith and public policy.

The show is built around plainspoken conversations with founders, investors, scientists, operators, public leaders and thinkers about what Australia can build, back and become.

What it follows

People doing serious work on the country's future.

Frontier technology: AI, quantum, space and advanced manufacturing.

National capability: energy, minerals, infrastructure, water and defence-adjacent industry.

Capital and ambition: founders, markets, institutions and Australian companies with global reach.

Culture and ethics: faith, work, education, human dignity and the social questions raised by technology.
